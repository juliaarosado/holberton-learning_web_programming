Rating SSBU Stages

banana

Final Destination

A classic. The lack of stage hazards and excessive platforms/structures allow for a fair playing field and the changing background looks sick. A go-to choice for parties or just chilling. 10/10

Battlefield

Another classic, but this time with platforms which aid mobility. Big Battlefield adds platforms which makes playing with 7 other people a lot easier if you can organize 7 people to be in the same place at the same time. I like the plants. 10/10

Bridge of Eldin

Basically Final Destination but with a minor stage hazard. Watching King Bulbin run across the map is all fun and games until you're not paying attention and die. The middle is a pain if you main someone without good recovery or projectiles. 8/10
